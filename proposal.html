Project Proposal

Project Title

'Pocket Money Tracker'

Project Overview

Pocket Money Tracker is the app that helps you to track your expenditures of your daily life. So that you can manage your expenditure.Firstly you need to declare your budget,in which way you want to expense your money. The way is pre-defined by this app,you can select these options.
Every Day you expense money and record it in your app with catagory.After the day you can easily track how much money you expense in that day. The app provide you a graphical interface.You can track record weekly and monthly as well.You can compare current month with previous month.
If your budget is insufficient ,the app gives you notification also.If your expense is grater than your budget ,it will be add your liabilities.

Project Objective

1.You must be declare your budget with time duration,it's may be weekly or mothly.
2.EveryDay you need to update your expeses.
3.You also need declare how many money you want to save.

Why you use this app?

1.You can track your expenditures.
2.Helps you to reduce waste expenses
3.It helps you to lead life with limited money.
4.Simple UI
5.Date time of an expense you can seen and compare with another.
